Proposed Solution for Manhole Issue at Ward 12, Davangere

Issue Description:
Manhole MH-045 on MG Road has a broken cover and collapsed brick masonry
near the inlet pipe. Sewage is overflowing onto the road during peak hours,
causing public health concerns and traffic disruption.

Proposed Solution:
1. Immediate desilting of the manhole using a super-suction vehicle.
2. Removal of damaged brick masonry and replacement with RCC precast
   cone segments (600mm diameter) for structural strength.
3. Install a new ductile iron cover (class D400) with locking arrangement
   to prevent theft and unauthorized opening.
4. Relay the inlet pipe (existing 200mm PVC) with a 300mm RCC NP2 pipe
   for increased flow capacity.
5. Apply anti-corrosive coating on all metal parts.
6. Restore road surface with hot mix asphalt around the manhole.

Timeline: 7 working days
Cost Estimate: ~₹45,000
Required Approvals: Assistant Engineer (AE) approval before work order
